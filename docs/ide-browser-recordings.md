## Browser Recordings

Every time the browser subagent actuates on the Browser, it may choose to generate a recording of the agent’s actions for your review. You can view this playback, if it is available, at the bottom of the Browser step UI.

![Browser Recording Capture Tool](https://res.cloudinary.com/dv3vzmogk/image/upload/v1781235862/aha-mind/docs-crawler/antigravity.google/browser-recording-capture_xlwl9s.png)

All browser recordings are also saved as a recording artifact for your review. This view loops through the browser agent’s actions.

![Browser Recording Artifact](https://res.cloudinary.com/dv3vzmogk/image/upload/v1781235862/aha-mind/docs-crawler/antigravity.google/browser-recording-artifact_camfp4.png)
